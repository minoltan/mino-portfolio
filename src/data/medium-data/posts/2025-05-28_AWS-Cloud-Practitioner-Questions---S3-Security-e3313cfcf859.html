Question 1:

Your client wants to make sure that file encryption is happening in S3, but he wants to fully manage the encryption keys and never store them in AWS. You recommend him to use ………………………

Correct Answer: (3) SSE-C is correct because it allows you to encrypt files in AWS while maintaining complete control over your encryption keys, ensuring they are never stored in AWS. This aligns perfectly with your client’s requirement to manage their own encryption keys independently.

Question 2:

A company you’re working for wants their data stored in S3 to be encrypted. They don’t mind the encryption keys stored and managed by AWS, but they want to maintain control over the rotation policy of the encryption keys. You recommend them to use ………………..

Correct Answer: (2) SSE-KMS is the correct choice because it allows AWS to manage your encryption keys while giving you control over the rotation policy. This meets the company’s requirement of having control over how often the keys are rotated, while still ensuring the keys are safely stored within AWS.

Question 3:

Your company does not trust AWS for the encryption process and wants it to happen on the application. You recommend them to use ………………..

Correct Answer: (4) Client-Side Encryption is correct because it allows you to handle the encryption process yourself, giving you full control over your encryption keys and ensuring AWS cannot access or decrypt your data. This approach aligns perfectly with your company’s trust concerns regarding AWS’s handling of encryption.

Question 4:

You have a website that loads files from an S3 bucket. When you try the URL of the files directly in your Chrome browser it works, but when a website with a different domain tries to load these files it doesn’t. What’s the problem?

Correct Answer: (3) CORS is crucial for allowing resources from one domain to be accessed by a website from another domain; since your files load directly in the browser but not from a different website, it indicates a CORS configuration issue. Understanding CORS helps ensure your web applications can effectively interact with resources stored in S3.

Question 5:

An e-commerce company has its customers and orders data stored in an S3 bucket. The company’s CEO wants to generate a report to show the list of customers and the revenue for each customer. Customer data stored in files on the S3 bucket has sensitive information that we don’t want to expose in the report. How do you recommend the report can be created without exposing sensitive information?

Correct Answer : (1) S3 Object Lambda allows you to modify the data as it is accessed, enabling you to remove sensitive information before it’s returned to the report generator, thus ensuring data privacy while still generating the necessary reports. This approach directly addresses the requirement of protecting sensitive customer data while delivering the desired output.

Question 6:

You suspect that some of your employees try to access files in an S3 bucket that they don’t have access to. How can you verify this is indeed the case without them noticing?

Correct Answer : (1) By enabling S3 Access Logs, you can capture detailed records of all requests made to your S3 buckets, which allows you to analyze potentially unauthorized access attempts using Amazon Athena. This method ensures that your monitoring remains discreet and doesn’t alert employees, aligning perfectly with your goal of verifying unauthorized access without their knowledge.

Question 7:

You are looking to provide temporary URLs to a growing list of federated users to allow them to perform a file upload on your S3 bucket to a specific location. What should you use?

Correct Answer : (2) URLs allow you to securely grant temporary access to specific actions in your S3 bucket, making them ideal for federated users to upload files without needing permanent credentials. This solution directly addresses the requirement for controlled access while ensuring security and convenience.

Question 8:

For compliance reasons, your company has a policy mandate that database backups must be retained for 4 years. It shouldn’t be possible to erase them. What do you recommend?

Correct Answer : (1) Solution provides immutability for your database backups, ensuring they cannot be deleted or altered for a specified retention period. This aligns perfectly with your company’s compliance requirements for retaining data for four years without the risk of premature deletion.

Question 9:

You would like all your files in an S3 bucket to be encrypted by default. What is the optimal way of achieving this?

Correct Answer : (2) S3 provides default encryption for new objects, ensuring your data is protected at rest without requiring any additional configuration. This aligns perfectly with the objective of securing all files in the bucket by default.

Question 10:

You have enabled versioning and want to be extra careful when it comes to deleting files on an S3 bucket. What should you enable to prevent accidental permanent deletions?

Correct Answer: (2) It adds an important layer of security by requiring users to provide multi-factor authentication (MFA) codes before deleting any S3 objects, thus helping to prevent accidental permanent deletions. This directly supports your goal of safely managing deletions in your S3 bucket and aligns perfectly with best practices for data protection.

Question 11:

A company has its data and files stored on some S3 buckets. Some of these files need to be kept for a predefined period of time and protected from being overwritten and deletion according to company compliance policy. Which S3 feature helps you in doing this?

Correct Answer: (3) It ensures that files cannot be deleted or overwritten for a specified retention period, which directly aligns with your company’s compliance requirements. This feature provides the necessary protection for your critical data, ensuring it is preserved as needed.

Question 12:

Which of the following S3 Object Lock configuration allows you to prevent an object or its versions from being overwritten or deleted indefinitely and gives you the ability to remove it manually?

Correct Answer: (3) It allows you to prevent an object and its versions from being overwritten or deleted indefinitely, while also giving you the option to remove the hold manually when necessary. This aligns with the objective of maintaining data integrity and compliance by ensuring critical data is protected from accidental loss.
