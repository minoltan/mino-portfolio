Introduction

Welcome to Episode 18 of our series on preparing for the AWS Certified Solutions Architect Associate (SAA-C03) certification! Whether you’re building a mobile app, a globally scalable website, or a microservices-based system, this blog provides a comprehensive guide with theory and hands-on steps to solidify your understanding of AWS services. Each section includes detailed explanations and actionable steps to implement these solutions using AWS services like API Gateway, Lambda, DynamoDB, S3, CloudFront, ECS, and more.
